This dataset shows the country profile of Nepal and disaster database.

Dataset summary:
  Composition of Disasters:
    THis databset contains various natural disasters such Accident, Avalance , epidemic, pollution and their effects such as: DataCards, deaths, injured, missing etc.
  Spatial Distribution
    This dataset contains the the impacts of natural disaster in the 5 development regions in Nepal.
  Temporal Behaviour 
    This dataset contains the nu of deaths, injured, missing, house destroyed, damaged and the no of them relocated, evacated, Losses in between year 1971 to 2013.
    
 Flood, earthquake and Fire were the top 3 causes of highest housing damages.
 Landslide, flood and epidemic were highest cause of mortality in between the year 1971 to 2013.
