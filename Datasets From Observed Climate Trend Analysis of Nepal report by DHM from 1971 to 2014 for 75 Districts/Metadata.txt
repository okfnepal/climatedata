	Additional information on data collection methods: Data is directly sourced from 93 climatic stations by DHM, Look under Data for more details.
	
2. Data processing methods 
	The outliers and unusual values were corrected for individual stations by replacing them by average of the value from previous day and the next day.
	More details can be found under methodology in the full report.

	Not Available 

4. Quality assurance procedures
	RClimdex software was used to test quality of the meterological data.

5. Analysis methods
	Trend and Extreme Index Analysis was done. Look under methodology for more details. 


************************
SUMMARY OF DATA FILES
************************

1. List of data files
	Filename: District Maximum Temperature Trends with Corresponding Significance Levels for 75 Districts
	Short description: Summary of district distribution with positive and negative trends annually and seasonally is given. Annual and seasonal maximum temperature trends for districts along with significance levels are annexed

	Filename: District Minimum Temperature Trends with Corresponding Significance Levels for 75 Districts
	Short description: The annual and seasonal minimum temperature trends for the districts are listed. Summary of districts with positive and negative trends for seasonal and annual minimum temperature trends is given

	Filename: District Precipitation Trends with Corresponding Significance Levels for 75 Districts
	Short description: The distribution of districts with positive and negative seasonal and annual precipitation trends is given in the dataset.

	Filename: District Trends for Extreme Climate Inidices for 75 Districts
	Short description: Results of trend analysis of eleven extreme climate indices for 75 districts of Nepal are presented in this dataset. The district trend values with significance levels for each climate are listed.
	
	Filename: Seasonal and Annual Climatic Normals for 5 Physiographic Regions
	Short description: Annual and seasonal normal are calculated by averaging each time series data (for precipitation, maximum temperature and minimum temperature) for the period from 1981 to 2010.
	
	Filename: Seasonal and Annual Climatic Normals for 75 districts
	Short description: Precipitation(in mm ), minimum/maximum temperatures (in degree celcius) normals for seasonal and annual time interval is expressed for each of five physiographic regions. These physiographic regions are divided on the basis of masl(meters above sea level).
	

2. Relationships between files
	None
